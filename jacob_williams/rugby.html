Rugby

Before coming to college I hardly knew what rugby was. However, I was approached by several team members during Camp Yale and decided to go out to the rugby pitch for a practice one day. I quickly fell in love with the game and have centered much of my life around it since. Yale's team is the oldest in the country and plays a full ivy league schedule as well as taking a yearly tour abroad.
